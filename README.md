ğŸ“ Problem Statement

The growing expansion of online marketplaces has created massive sales data, but without proper analysis, businesses struggle to identify when sales are highest, which products perform best, and which regions contribute the most revenue.
To support business growth and strategic decision-making, a clear and dynamic insight dashboard is required.

This project aims to tackle the following challenges:

Identify best-selling products and key revenue drivers.

Determine peak sales periods (month & year) for better forecasting.

Analyze regional and category-wise sales performance.

Understand city-wise product demand distribution.

Present insights through a visually appealing, interactive Power BI dashboard.

By transforming raw e-commerce data into meaningful business intelligence visuals, this dashboard enables leadership teams to improve planning, inventory management, and marketing focus with data-driven decisions.
